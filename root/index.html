The Sony situation

Homecoming, Far From Home, No Way Home, and Brand New Day are MCU canon but Sony controls distribution — they rotate on and off Disney+ (historically Netflix, then Starz) instead of living there permanently. They still belong exactly where they're placed in the order. Sony's Venom/Morbius/Madame Web/Kraven universe is not MCU canon and isn't included here.
